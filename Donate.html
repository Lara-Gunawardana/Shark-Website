Donate

Sharks are vital to healthy oceans, yet many species are facing serious threats from overfishing and habitat loss. By donating to shark conservation organizations, you help fund research, protection efforts, and advocacy that keep these incredible animals — and our oceans — thriving for future generations.

Shark Trust

The Shark Trust is a UK-based charity working globally to safeguard the future of sharks, rays and their relatives through science, education, advocacy, and real-world conservation action. They champion species protection, sustainable fisheries management, and responsible trade to help threatened marine populations thrive. By supporting policy change, citizen science projects, and community engagement, they’re driving positive change for our oceans and the remarkable creatures that keep them healthy. Your donation can make a real difference in protecting sharks and the ecosystems they sustain — every contribution helps secure a brighter, healthier future for our seas.

Shark Allies

Shark Allies is a dedicated nonprofit working to protect sharks, rays, and the ocean ecosystems they depend on by raising awareness, advocating for better policy, and energizing new voices for conservation. They support creative campaigns, leverage media and public engagement, and build momentum to end destructive practices like overfishing and the shark fin trade. With a lean, volunteer-driven approach and community-focused initiatives, Shark Allies helps shift perceptions and policy in favor of these vital marine species. Your donation fuels their advocacy, outreach, and action — helping ensure healthier oceans and brighter futures for sharks worldwide.

Ocearch

OCEARCH is a global nonprofit that leads groundbreaking shark and ocean research expeditions, gathering critical scientific data to help restore ocean balance and inform effective conservation strategies. They share open-source tracking data and educational resources that inspire students, scientists, and the public to better understand and protect these vital marine species. By supporting their work — from field science to public outreach — your donation fuels discovery and helps ensure healthier, more abundant oceans for future generations. Every contribution makes a difference in advancing research, education, and conservation action.

The Big Shark Pledge

The Big Shark Pledge is a powerful campaign by the Shark Trust to unite global voices in calling for stronger, science-based protection for oceanic sharks and rays facing steep declines due to unsustainable high seas fishing. By signing the pledge, you add your voice to one of the biggest ever advocacy efforts for shark conservation — helping put pressure on governments and fisheries to act and safeguard these incredible species. Your signature helps build a louder, united call for change to ensure sharks thrive in healthy oceans for generations to come. Join the movement and sign the Big Shark Pledge today — every voice counts in the fight for our seas.

sign the petition

Stand with ocean life by signing the petition urging the President of the Maldives to continue the ban on fishing all shark species and protect these vital marine predators. Sharks play essential roles in maintaining healthy reefs, supporting biodiversity, and sustaining nature-based tourism that benefits local communities. Add your voice to this effort for long-term ocean health — every signature helps amplify the call for sustainable, shark-friendly policies and environmental stewardship.
